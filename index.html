OUR SPACES

Meet. Train. Play. At Novotel Edinburgh Park, the ultimate 4-star venue for all your
meeting and event needs.
Our well-equipped event spaces can accommodate parties of up to 180 guests.
Facilities include experienced event staff, high speed WiFi access, fully air-
conditioned rooms and high quality audio visual equipment.
Located next to Edinburgh Park train station and directly off the M8, Novotel
Edinburgh Park benefits from exceptional access. Edinburgh Airport and City Centre
can be accessed via regular buses or trams.
